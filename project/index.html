Rei Ayanami

Nobody really knows where Rei comes from (with maybe the exception of Gendo Ikari).The first time she appeared, she was described by Gendo Ikari as one of his friend’s daughter and that he was only taking care of her. She lives by herself in a dull sparsely-furnished apartment in Tokyo-3 . Toward the end of the series, Shinji comments that her apartment is very similar to the place where Ritsuko says Rei was born. Rei is also shown to have an interest in psychology and genetics, including reading a book in German on the latter subject in Episode 09 .

Incarnations

During the course of the series, Rei dies twice.She is replaced by Gendo with another clone. She’s aware that she is replaceable as she often says this when fighting the fourteenth angel. Rei III is aware that she is the third one.
